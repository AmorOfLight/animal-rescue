About Animal Rescue SA

Animal Rescue SA is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to the welfare and protection of animals across South Africa. Since our establishment, we have worked tirelessly to create a better world for animals in need through rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming efforts.

Our Mission

We strive to be the voice for the voiceless, working to end animal suffering through direct rescue, comprehensive care, and community education. Our mission extends beyond immediate rescue to create lasting change in how society views and treats animals.

Compassion

We treat every animal with love and respect

Dedication

Committed to providing the best care possible

Community

Working together to create positive change

Our Journey

Foundation

1999

Established with a mission to rescue and rehabilitate animals in need across South Africa.

Expansion of Services

2005-2015

Grew our operations to include mobile veterinary services, education programs, and expanded shelter facilities.

Current Impact

2015-Present

Successfully rehomed thousands of animals and established comprehensive community support programs.

Today, Animal Rescue SA continues to grow and adapt to meet the changing needs of animals in our community. With the support of our dedicated volunteers, staff, and donors, we remain committed to our mission of creating a better world for animals in need.
